About us

In February 2005 a group of eight Jamaicans residing in the Geneva area met at the Jamaica Mission offices in Geneva to plan a Bob Marley fête. The year 2005 is the 60th anniversary of the birth of the musical icon from Jamaica who brought reggae music to the world. The group worked for three months and on Saturday, May 12th, 245 Jamaicans and friends rocked until 03h00 on Sunday morning to reggae music, ate sumptuous Jamaican food and washed it down with Red Stripe beer and Jamaican rum punch. The fete was a success.
