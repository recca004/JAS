About us

In February 2005 a group of eight Jamaicans residing in the Geneva area met at the Jamaica Mission offices in Geneva to plan a fete in tribute to Bob Marley fête. This meeting became the defining moment when the decision was made to also start an association for Jamaicans and friends of Jamaica, in Switzerland.
